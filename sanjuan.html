San Juan, Puerto Rico

repl.it

I went to San Juan, Puerto Rico in December of 2019. My family and family friends went on a Carriean cruise together, and one of the stops was San Juan!

Why I love San Juan

  1. Developing area (less reliant on technology). A new kind of city I am not used to.
  2. The houses are beautiful! They are colorful
  3. The culture there is different
  4. Amazing sight seeing areas, like their trees, ocean and nature.
